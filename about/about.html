About us

Turn Out is an attendance management system which includes Student's attendance, Student details
like name, Roll number, DOB, sex, branch, semester, phone number, address. Professor's attendance and their details like Professor ID, name, department, and etc.,
non-Faculty attendance and their details like NF ID, Name, Profession phone no. and etc.,
Student Faculty attendance and details like ID, name, department and etc. It also allows user to
access calendar which has< details like events, occasions and vacation that will
happen in the college. The aim of case study is to design and develop a
database for the attendance management to maintain the record of students, professors and other staff's attendance. It also shows the total number of working
days and holidays of the college and gives a reminder to Student if their attendance is below 75%.
